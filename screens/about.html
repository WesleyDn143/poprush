Our Story

PopRush is a flavored popcorn brand born from a simple obsession: why should snacks be boring? We started in 2024 with a mission to bring high-energy flavors to students, movie lovers, and anyone who craves a snack that actually hits different.

Our Mission

To deliver highly craved, boldly flavored, and ridiculously affordable snacks that turn every boring moment into a main event. We believe every crunch should be an experience.

Our Vision

We see a world where no one settles for bland. We're here to redefine the snack aisle with relentless energy, premium ingredients, and flavors that absolutely pop off.
